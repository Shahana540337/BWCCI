President's Message

Welcome to the web page of Bangladesh Women Chamber of Commerce & Industry, a platform to unite and channel the enormous energy, innovations, and opportunities of the women entrepreneurs of Bangladesh. BWCCI is the first ever Chamber of Commerce in Bangladesh, created solely with the aim of achieving economic empowerment of women. The dream was born in June 2001, through the strong leadership of Selima Ahmad
